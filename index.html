Our Tools

Aging Stock Merge (PSM-NUK)

Merge PSM and NUK aging stock reports to get a consolidated view of stock age across both sources. Includes custom aging buckets and export options.

Stock Merger Dashboard

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of merged stock data from PSM and NUK files. Features include search, GSL filtering, stock threshold filters, and multiple export options.

Invoice Generator

Quickly generate proforma or final invoices. Features include dynamic item adding, automatic calculations, and export to PDF or XLSX.

Aging With Price & Vendor

A custom tool to manage aging stock, potentially incorporating price and vendor information. Specific functionalities depend on its internal implementation.

Floorstock Management

An interface to track, manage, and update floorstock levels in real-time. Streamline inventory control and reporting directly from the floor.
